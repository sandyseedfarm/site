Sandy Seed Farm

Our Story

Sandy Seed Farm is a biodynamic peri-urban farm located in Montrose, Colorado. We utilize plots of land for food production that would have become (or remained!) landscaped. By using organic growing methods and minimal tillage, we transform unproductive spaces into community food assets. This food is produced in a way that enhances biodiversity and increases soil health, all while providing a beautiful space for the community to value as their own.

What is a CSA?

Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) program is a relationship between a farm committed to growing food sustainably and a community of farm friends committed to eating locally and supporting local farmers.
